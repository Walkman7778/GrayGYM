Core Purpose of the Application

Gray_GYM is a specialized cognitive health platform designed to help users maintain and improve their mental agility. The application's primary goal is to provide a comprehensive environment for memory training, attention development, and logical thinking through interactive, evidence-based neuro-games.

Functionality & Features

Gray_GYM offers a suite of tools for cognitive self-improvement, allowing users to track their progress and compete in global mental challenges:

  • Stroop Test: Training cognitive flexibility and interference control.
  • Schulte Tables: Developing speed reading and peripheral visual attention.
  • Memory Sequences: Exercises for strengthening short-term working memory.
  • Word Games: Verbal logic and vocabulary expansion tasks.
  • Global Leaderboards: Real-time competitive ranking with players worldwide.

User Data & Transparency

To ensure data integrity and a seamless user experience, Gray-GYM requests specific data for the following purposes:

  • Google OAuth Identity: We use your Google profile only to uniquely identify your game account and protect your personal records from unauthorized access.
  • Cloud Synchronization: Your scores, unlocked levels, and training statistics are stored securely in the cloud to allow you to resume your progress on any mobile device.
  • Phone Authentication (OTP): This provides an additional layer of security to verify account ownership and prevent data loss.

Transparency Note: We do not access your contacts, files, or any personal data not directly related to game progress. We never share your data with third-party advertisers.
